About me

I'm currently pursuing my PhD degree under the guidance of Prof Qi Ye , Shibo He and Jiming Chen (Zhejiang University). Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in 2019 from China University of Geosciences (Wuhan).

Research interests

My prior research interests lie in the autonomous 3D reconstruction of scenes, including:

  • Online 3D Reconstruction (NeRF / 3DGS)
  • View Path Planning (Single Robot / Multiple Robots)
  • Open-Vocabulary 3D Scene Understanding and Object Completion (CLIP, Diffusion)

My current research interests focus on embodied intelligence applications empowered by 3D perception, including:

  • Vision-Language Navigation (VLN)
  • Language-driven Object Grasp Detection
  • Mobile Manipulation
